Pegaso Ciudad del Carmen, Campeche




Gulf of Mexico Flight Operations

Located in a strategic point in the beautiful southeast of Mexico, our Gulf of Mexico Flight Operations center at the International Airport of Ciudad del Carmen, Campeche State includes offices, a warehouse and 2 hangars for maintenance and aircraft shelter. There is a customs office and a permanent training center for our operation and maintenance personnel onsite. The total area is over 2,200 square meters.

We have all the necessary certification and authorizations from the Airworthiness Authorities (DGAC) for the operation of an aeronautical workshop for the maintenance of gliders, helicopters, engines and accessories. We have a land team for the transportation of personnel and also for the support services needed for the aerial team. (Airworthiness Authorization nº 24, 51 and f20, respectively).
