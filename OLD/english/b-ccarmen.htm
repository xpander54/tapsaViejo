Located in a strategic point in the beautiful southeast of Mexico, Pegaso counts with two hangars in the International Airport of Ciudad del Carmen, Campeche State, for maintenance jobs and aircraft shelter, additionally to offices and a storehouse. The area has a 2,200 m2 surface.

Since 1985 we count with all authorizations necessaries from Airworthiness Authorities (DGAC) for the operation of an aeronautical workshop for the maintenance of gliders, helicopters, engines and accessories. We have a terrestrial team for the transportation of personnel and also for the services necessaries to support the aerial team. Moreover in these offices there is a custom office and a permanent training center for our operation and maintenance personnel with highly qualified instructors (Airworthiness Authorization nº 24, 51 and f20, respectively).
