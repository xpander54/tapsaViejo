The Central Office of Transportes Aereos Pegaso is located in the south of the city, street Santander nº15, Col. Insurgentes Mixcoac, at only 30 minutes from the International Airport of Mexico City “Benito Juarez” and 50 minutes from the International Airport of Toluca.

This important site is equipped with the advanced technology “frame relay”, system of communication 24 hours a day through a virtual circuit that allows the control and management of all the other operation sites, through a network, data, voice and video.

The software installed in the Central Office has been designed in accordance with the necessities of our company to optimize every type of operation. The computer system is also linked through a private network in accordance with the specific job of our personnel.
