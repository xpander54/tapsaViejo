Transportes Aereos Pegaso, S. A. de C. V., was constituted in 1981 and through a successful trajectory of 26 years it has become the company leader in aerial transportation in Mexico, to the height of the expectations of the globalized world of century XXI.

Technically and professionally we integrate an excellent work group with modern infrastructure and knowledge of high level for the maintenance of our aircraft and the qualification of our personnel.

Two years after the beginning of Transportes Aereos Pegaso operations, in 1983, the company obtained the first contract to work for Petróleos Mexicanos in the transportation of passengers and shipment outside the coast, demonstrating its operational capacity and forging a solid knowledge in the branch.

 

Up to 2007 we have conducted more than 540,000 operations, with 115,000 flight hours, transporting more than 1,350,000 passengers. Every year we perform perfectly more than 42.000 operations and make arrive at their destiny to near 112.000 people.

In Transportes Aereos Pegaso, we fulfill with the highest standards of security and quality according to the effective international norms and based in our values.
   


To offer a safe service and with quality in Aerial Transportation.
   


To do of the private flights, or for executives, for tourists or for enterprise operations, a way of life, that besides satisfying to the maximum all the clients' necessities, serves to impel the economic and social development of Mexico.

   


Our values guarantee our work: responsibility, order and knowledge.
