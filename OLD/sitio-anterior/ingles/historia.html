History

 

Pegaso Air Transportation began operation in 1981. Since 1983, we have had a contract with Petroleos Mexicanos to provide service of transporting their employees to and from the ocean based oil stations. Beginning in August of 2003, 50,000 hours of flying time was logged over the sea.
Up to the present date, there have been several operations of alpine rescue, fire fighting, cargo transport, sea rescues, and emergency ambulance transport.
