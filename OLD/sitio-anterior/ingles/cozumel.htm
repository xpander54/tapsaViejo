A fascinating trip with magnificent and beautiful aerial views to appreciate Cozumel Island: its crystal clear water beaches, white sand, exuberant vegetation and spectacular sceneries. We are certain that you will enjoy it enormously!

Cozumel - Tulum - Cozumel

In the middle of the Riviera Maya you will find one of the most fascinanting and interesting archaeological zone on the coast of the Caribbean Sea; during your flight you will appreciate, "Xel-Ha" a national park considered to be the largest natural aquarium in the world with its fresh water rivers and numerous cenotes, also you will see coral reefs and rocky shores and peaceful white sand beaches. Throughout this unforgettable journey you will be constantly amazed by the region´s natural wonder. There is a high level of energy here filling us with vitality and peace. It is an spectacular tour!

Flight time: 50 minutes

Cozumel - Cobá - Cozumel

While flying west across the ocean you will enjoy the spectacular view of the array of blue tones of the Mexican Caribbean and the presence of a dense tropical jungle until reaching an exotic, magical, and sultry Cobá, one of the greatest sites constructed by the Mayas. An unforgettable trip!

Flight Time : 70 minutes
