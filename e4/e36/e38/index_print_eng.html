Our Cessna 206 has a capacity of 5 passengers and features a luxurious interior and very comfortable business class seats. Nicknamed "the SUV of the air" by pilots around the world, the 206 is often used for aerial photography, skydiving or as a rugged bush plane in Australia, Africa and the Amazon.
