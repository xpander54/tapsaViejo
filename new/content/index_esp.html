Transportes Aéreos Pegaso

Transportes Aereos Pegaso has been providing the finest in private air charter transportation for over 26 years now. We deliver the high level of service required in today’s demanding business environment. Pegaso specializes in executive flights and the transportation of personnel to the offshore oil and gas fields in the Gulf of Mexico. We offer a wide range of aircraft and services to best fit your needs - business or pleasure. Our commitment to safety and excellence has earned us respect throughout the air transportation industry. We are proud to be the first Mexican aviation company to have obtained ISO 9001:2000 certification.
