Nicknamed the Eco-Star, the Eurocopter 130 B4 is perfect for tourist flights and business travel because of its high comfort and low noise. It is one of the quietest helicopters in the world because of the patented Fenestron encased tail rotor system. It has an extraordinarily comfortable executive configuration and seats 7 passengers.
