The exclusive Learjet 31A is a cutting-edge aircraft designed by Bombardier AeroSpace. The roomy cabin seats 8 passengers in luxurious comfort. With a cruising speed of 506 mph (814 km/h) you can be sure of making good time. Another plus: there is plenty of luggage storage space available, which is not always the case with jets of this class.
