Pegaso Mexico, Distrito Federal




Our Headquarters

The Main Office of Transportes Aereos Pegaso is located to the south of downtown Mexico City, only 30 minutes from the International Airport of Mexico City “Benito Juarez” and 50 minutes from the International Airport of Toluca.

This high-tech site is equipped with the advanced technology “frame relay”, system of communication that allows the 24 hour real-time control and management of all the other operation sites, using a network, data, voice and video.

The software installed in the Central Office has been specially designed for Pegaso to optimize daily business.
