Security/Safety


At Transportes Aereos Pegaso, we fulfill the highest standards of security and quality based on international aviation norms. We are proud to be the first Mexican company to have obtained the ISO 9001:2000 certification in the field of aviation. Our aircraft are maintained according to the most stringent international and manufacturer guidelines.

Pegaso was also the first aerial transportation company to develop specialized software to regulate and register every aspect of every flight. This helps us to improve service and maximizes flight safety and pilot training.
