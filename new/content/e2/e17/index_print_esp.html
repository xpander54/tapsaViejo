Team/Personal


Professional and highly qualified pilots are one of the most important factors in our success. Our pilots, maintenance technicians, and support personnel are all committed to excellence in safety and service. We employ a comprehensive system of ongoing training for both our pilots and mechanics in order to keep them up-to-date on every system for every situation.

Pegaso was the first aerial transportation company to develop specialized software to regulate and register every aspect of every flight. This helps us improve service and maximizes flight safety and pilot training.
