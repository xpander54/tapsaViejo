About Us


Transportes Aereos Pegaso, S. A. de C. V.

Transportes Aereos Pegaso, S. A. de C. V., was founded in 1981. Driven by a commitment to safety and reliability, we are now the market leader in aerial transportation in Mexico. Our clients throughout the Americas look to us to help them meet the demands of the globalized business world. Our know-how is built on a commitment to hard work and rock solid experience.

Our pilots, maintenance technicians, and support personnel are all committed to one thing: excellence in safety and service. At Transportes Aereos Pegaso, we fulfill the highest standards of security and quality based on the international industry norms. We are proud to be the first Mexican aviation to have obtained the prestigious ISO 9001:2000 certification.
