Why should I fly in a private jet instead of taking a commercial flight?

A private flight brings you a lot of advantages that you simply just can’t have with commercial flights.

• You can choose the exact hour of your departure to best fit your schedule. You don’t have to arrive at the airport 2-hours ahead of departure.

• Only you and your colleagues share the flight. You won’t have to deal with the aggravation of strange passengers.

• With a private jet you also have access to smaller regional airports where large commercial planes can’t land. This puts you closer to your destination and saves you even more time.

• The exclusive level of service you receive from our team and at our facilities is much higher and costs less than you would pay at a commercial provider.

Are private flights much more expensive than commercial flights?

On the contrary! Our business jet seats up to 8 passengers, and the cost is much less when you compare it with buying 8 tickets for Business or First Class on a commercial carrier. Just ask for a quote!

How many airports can Pegaso aircraft fly into?

Any airport in Mexico, the United States, Central or South America that is licensed from their local airworthiness authorities agreement to operate as a National or International airport.
